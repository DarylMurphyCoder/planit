About PlanIt!

PlanIt! helps you organize tasks clearly, stay focused, and ship on time.

What PlanIt! does

  • Fast task capture with title, description, priority, due date, and category.
  • Filter and search by status, priority, category, and keywords.
  • Mark tasks complete with a single click and keep progress visible.
  • Responsive, accessible UI that works great on desktop and mobile.

Why it's useful

  • Reduces context switching by keeping all work in one place.
  • Clarifies priorities so you focus on what matters next.
  • Encourages momentum with simple, fast interactions.
  • Secure and production-ready with Heroku deployment.
